Don't Let Chuck Norris Kick You In The Face

Wins: Times Kicked in Face:

Don't be fooled.

Chuck Norris, our country's greatest hero, is most certainly lurking around here somewhere. Just because you can't see him doesn't mean he isn't there.

It's a well known fact that Chuck is good at kicking stuff, but what you might not know is that Chuck absolutely hates it when he comes up with a random word or phrase about himself and people can't guess all the letters in the word or phrase. It makes him so mad that when someone guesses a wrong letter enough times, he kicks that someone in the face. You don't want that to happen to you.

Chuck has come up with a word/phrase for you that has to do with... well, Chuck. Use the keyboard to guess the letters or numbers in it, but beware: every time you guess incorrectly, Chuck reveals himself a bit more, and if you guess wrong , he will be upon you... and will most likely kick you in the face.
